Privacy Policy (Scheduling Feature)

This Privacy Policy explains how EduPlanner collects, uses, and protects information related to its scheduling feature. By using this feature, you agree to the practices outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

EduPlanner collects scheduling-related data such as teacher names, assigned subjects, time slots, section identifiers, and workload hours. We do not collect unnecessary personal information unrelated to scheduling.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used solely for the purpose of generating, displaying, and managing class schedules. It helps ensure accurate teacher assignments and conflict-free schedules.

3. Data Access and Permissions

Only authorized users such as school administrators and designated schedulers have access to scheduling data. Access is role-based and password-protected.

4. Data Sharing

EduPlanner does not share or sell scheduling data to any third parties. All information is treated as confidential and used exclusively within your institution’s account.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your scheduling data from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.
